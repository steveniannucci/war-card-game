Rules

It's like a typical game of War.

The player who claims all the cards wins.

Each time you draw a card, I will also be dealt one. Then, whichever one of us has the better card will also claim the loser's. The winner of the each match will place the cards they claimed into their own seperate deck which we'll get to use after we've exhausted the original one.

If we both happen to draw the same rank, we'll place three more cards into our war stacks. The third or last card in the stack will determine who takes all the cards in the current match. If the last cards are the same rank again, we'll reinitiate the war, adding onto the existing stack. The winner takes everything.

One more thing, Jacks are the weakest rank. You'll have to enter War with another Jack if you draw it to win.
